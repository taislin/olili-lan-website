anena

mountain



akali etava anena

[the] bird goes [to the] mountain



ako

person


ako etoki

person speaks/says


ako etoki: "akali etava anena"

person says: "the bird goes to the mountain"



koko-ako etoki poka kaka-ako

[the] man speaks with [the] woman


  

koko-ako vo kaka-ako etava anena

[the] man and [the] woman go [to the] mountain


  

ako vo akali na-etava anena

[the] man and [the] bird do not go [to the] mountain
