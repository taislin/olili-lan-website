epana lan asona, tatono-ti (8)

Vocabulary I

 



ako ekala son atelo.

The person swims.






ako na-ekali. ako son atela.

The person does not fly. The person is in the ground.






akali ekali osoli.

The bird flies fast.







Summary
ekala to swim
ekali to fly
atela land/ground
atelo water
osoli fast
son in/at/on
