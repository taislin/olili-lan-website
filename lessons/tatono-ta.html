Past, Future

 



akolopo-ako pi-eko akala

the group of people had [a] fish






akolopo-ako pi-elokin akasi

the group of people saw [a] plant







akolopo-ako ni-eko akala

the group of people will have [a] fish






akolopo-ako ni-elokin akasi

the group of people will see [a] plant






Summary
eko to have
elokin to see
pi- past prefix
ni- future prefix
