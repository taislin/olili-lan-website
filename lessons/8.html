epana lan asona, tatono-ti (8)

Vocabulary I


ako ekala son atelo.

The person swims.


ako na-ekali. ako son atela.

The person does not fly. The person is in the ground.


akali ekali osoli.

The bird flies fast.


ako eli son avila.

The man is at home.


kaka-ako etoki: "ip lan olili-lan".

The woman says: "My language is olili-lan"


koko-ako etoki: "i epilin opona".

The man says: "I feel good".



Summary

ekala to swim
ekali to fly
atela land/ground
atelo water
osoli fast
son in/at/on
eli to be
epilin to feel
avila house/home
lan language
opona good
