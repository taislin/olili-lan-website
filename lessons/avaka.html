These 12 lessons were made with the aim of teaching olili-lan's 104 core words and all the relevant grammar.
Also included are reading, writing, listening and speaking exercises.
By the end, you will have acquired about 2/3 of olili-lan's vocabulary.
If you are interested in learning more, check the Dictionary and Grammar.
