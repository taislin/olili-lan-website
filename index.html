olili-lan learning center

Welcome to the world of olili-lan! olili-lan is a conlang (constructed language) intended to be a small, minimalist, and easy to learn. It is not meant to be a complex, naturalistic and specific language.
It has a reduced dictionary on purpose (less than 200 root words), trying to balance simplicity and communication potential. olili-lan is mainly influenced by tokipona and ygyde, with some extra bits of English, Portuguese, South Pacific Pidgins, and Hawaiian. It is also a philosophical and abstract language, styled after the Sapir-Whorf hypothesis.
